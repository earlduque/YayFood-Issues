# YayFood 1.2.0 Release Notes

## üéâ Major New Features

### Group Functionality (Beta)
YayFood now supports collaborative food tracking with friends and family! Share your restaurant discoveries and dish ratings in real-time with group members.

#### Getting Started with Groups
- **Create Groups**: Start a new group and invite friends with a shareable invite code
- **Join Groups**: Join existing groups using invite codes
- **Leave Groups**: Exit groups when needed
- **Group Details**: View all members with role indicators (admin/member)

#### Sharing Your Food Journey
- **Onboarding Sharing Wizard**: When joining a group, choose which restaurants and dishes to share
- **Share Controls**: Toggle sharing on/off for individual restaurants and dishes
- **Visual Indicators**: Blue group badges show which items are shared
- **Real-Time Updates**: See changes instantly when group members add or modify content
- **Photo Sharing**: Dish photos automatically sync across all group members' devices

#### Group Content Management
- **Browse Shared Content**: View all shared restaurants and dishes in a group
- **Delete Management**: Remove individual dishes or entire restaurants (admin only)
- **Automatic Sync**: Your data stays up-to-date across all your devices

#### Authentication Options
- **Email/Password**: Traditional account creation
- **Offline Mode**: Continue using the app without an account - all features work offline
- **Multi-Account Support**: Sign in with different accounts on the same device

_Note: Google Sign-In is temporarily disabled in this release._

### Enhanced Card Export Layouts
Share your food discoveries on social media with beautiful, customizable card exports:

- **Hero Score Layout**: Export single dishes with massive centered ratings and 5-star visualization
- **Intelligent Restaurant Collages**: Showcase 1-16 dishes from your favorite restaurants with smart grid layouts
- **Adaptive Photo Grids**: The app automatically selects the best grid layout for your photo count and card size
- **Top-Rated Priority**: Your highest-rated dishes get extra visual space with prominent top-row placement
- **Customizable Photo Count**: Choose exactly how many dishes to include (1-16 or "All available")
- **Big Score Overlays**: Large, easy-to-read ratings (perfect 10s display as "10" instead of "10.0")
- **Improved Photo Visibility**: Reduced overlays let your food photos shine through
- **Style Options**: Choose between "Big score" (default) or "Compact chips" layouts
- **Multiple Sizes**: Square (1:1), Post (4:5), and Story (9:16) formats for any platform

### Tap-to-Preview Export Cards
- **Interactive Preview**: Tap the placeholder to generate and preview cards before sharing
- **Visual Confidence**: See exactly what you'll share before committing
- **Setting Adjustments**: Change size/privacy settings and regenerate preview on the fly
- **Instant Actions**: "Save to Photos" and "Share" buttons reuse existing preview

### Simplified Analytics
Refocused analytics on what matters most - your ratings:

- **Top Rated Section**: View your top 5 restaurants and dishes by rating
- **Rating Distribution**: Visual breakdown showing excellent/great/good/average/poor ratings
- **Family Insights**: Per-person analytics with favorite restaurants and dishes (when using family ratings)
- **Recent Activity**: Recently rated dishes with relative timestamps
- **Combined Insights**: Discovery and rating insights in one place

**Streamlined Experience**: Removed visit/order frequency tracking to focus on rating-based insights. Your existing frequency data is preserved and can be restored if needed.

### Automatic Data Cleanup
YayFood now automatically keeps your data organized:

- **Duplicate Removal**: The app finds and merges duplicate restaurants and dishes automatically
- **Safe Merging**: All your ratings, photos, and notes are preserved when duplicates are combined
- **Always Running**: Cleanup happens in the background - you don't need to do anything

### Assign Unknown Ratings
If you see ratings marked as "Unknown," you can now assign them to the right family member:

- **Easy to Spot**: Unknown ratings have an orange badge
- **Quick Assignment**: Tap the + icon to choose which family member rated the dish
- **Smart Suggestions**: The app suggests the right person if names match
- **Legacy Support**: Perfect for cleaning up old ratings from before you started using groups
